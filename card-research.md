# Crypto Debit Card Research - NEXUS Debit Division

This report summarizes the top 3 crypto debit card providers for the NEXUS Debit Division landing page, including a premium No-KYC alternative.

## 1. RedotPay
**Best for: Instant Global Use & Apple Pay**

*   **Website:** [redotpay.com](https://www.redotpay.com)
*   **Apple/Google Pay:** Supported
*   **Cashback:** 0.5% up to 5% (promotional)
*   **Fees:**
    *   Virtual Card: $10
    *   Physical Card: $100
    *   Monthly Fee: $0
    *   Transaction Fee: 1%
*   **KYC:** Full KYC required (ID + Selfie)
*   **Supported Regions:** Global (Excluding USA, Mainland China)
*   **Unique Selling Points:** Instant virtual card generation, high success rates for subscriptions (Netflix, ChatGPT), and seamless mobile wallet integration.

## 2. Crypto.com (Visa Card)
**Best for: Ecosystem Rewards & Premium Perks**

*   **Website:** [crypto.com/cards](https://crypto.com/cards)
*   **Apple/Google Pay:** Supported
*   **Cashback:** 1% - 5% (Requires CRO staking)
*   **Fees:**
    *   Issuance: $0 (but requires CRO lockup)
    *   Monthly Fee: $0
    *   ATM: Free up to monthly limits
*   **KYC:** Full KYC required
*   **Supported Regions:** US, EU, UK, Canada, Singapore, Australia, Brazil
*   **Unique Selling Points:** Metal cards, reimbursement for Spotify/Netflix, airport lounge access, and integrated exchange ecosystem.

## 3. Bitrefill Card (The Elite No-KYC Option)
**Best for: Privacy & Direct Lightning Spending**

*   **Website:** [bitrefill.com/card](https://www.bitrefill.com/card/)
*   **Apple/Google Pay:** Supported
*   **Cashback:** 1% (Paid in Bitcoin Sats)
*   **Fees:**
    *   Issuance: $0 (Virtual)
    *   Monthly Fee: $0
    *   Transaction: Competitive conversion rates
*   **KYC:** **Partial / No-KYC** (Requires only Email and Phone for basic limits)
*   **Supported Regions:** EU/EEA (Expanding globally)
*   **Unique Selling Points:** Highest privacy level, spend directly from any BTC/Lightning wallet, no traditional bank account linkage required.
